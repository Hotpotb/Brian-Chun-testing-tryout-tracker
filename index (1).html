✅ Selected for the team
Meet with a club representative regarding next steps and registration. Some players selected on the first day may be asked to return for a second skate.
🔄 Called back
Players will return the following day for an additional evaluation skate.
❌ Released from tryouts
Players may continue attending tryouts with other organizations.
🏒 If your player is selected Immediately following tryouts, players and parents/guardians will meet with a club representative in the designated area. A Letter of Intent (LOI) will be completed at that time. Once signed, players may not participate in tryouts for another organization. Financial arrangements and registration must be completed online within 24 hours of team selection. Failure to complete registration prior to the team's next tryout session may result in the player's roster spot being released.
